Target Audience

The audience Weather Dot Com targets are residents or visitors of Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. Users in between the ages of 14-80 in the income range of $0-$100,000 and typically with easy access to a phone, tablet, or computer. Users will be drawn to this website as they seek to be involved in outdoor activities.

Personas

Jason:

Jason is a 26 year-old man who works in the construction industry. He tends to travel to southeast Idaho a lot for work, and usually likes to know what weather to expect so he can pack accordingly.

Angie:

Angie is a 47 year old stay-at-home mother and has 3 teenagers. Angie and her 3 kids love the oudoors and, especially in the summertime, they take advantage of the great weather in Idaho and plan a variety of outdoor activities at least once a week. In order for them to be 100% prepared for their activities, they make it a priority to check the weather before going on any adventure.

Scenarios

  • Should I bring a coat?
  • Should I leave earlier to the airport in order to get there on time in case there's bad weather?
  • Should I wear boots or tennis shoes?
  • Can we go on a hike or is it going to rain?
  • Is it going to be snowing this week?
  • Should I pack warmer clothes?
  • How much longer do we have of good weather for outdoor activities?
