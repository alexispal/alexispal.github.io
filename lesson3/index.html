Site Purpose

The purpose of Weather Dot Com is to facilitate weather informations to locals in Preston, Soda Springs, and Fish Haven. This site will also feature a Storm Center in which users can find more detailed information on storms happening in the area. A Gallery of pictures of each of those locations will be available as well, to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages and the towns themselves. Weather Dot Com will be accessible on all classes of devices such as computers, tablets and smartphones.
