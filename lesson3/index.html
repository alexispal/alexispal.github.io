Site Purpose

The purpose of Weather Dot Com is to facilitate weather informations to locals in Preston, Soda Springs, Fish Haven, and Storm Center. This site will also feature a Gallery of pictures of each of those locations to reflect the current weather condition data on the town pages and the towns themselves. Weather Dot Com will be accessible on all classes of devices such as computers, tablets and smartphones.
