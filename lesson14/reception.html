Reception Services

We are glad you chose us to accompany and assist you in your special day!

Remember that we have different types and sizes of rooms, perfect for each person in your party. Make sure to look for the perfect ones for your family and friends.

If you're planning on having a wedding reception at any of our locations, be sure to make reservations at least three months before the day of the wedding to avoid any inconveniences.

Here's a list of the different services available in all of our locations:

  • Changing rooms
  • Bathroom facilities
  • Baby-sitter services
  • Long-term stay accomodations
  • Reception and sitting area
  • Catering and food services
  • Family Search Center
